The Forest Trial

2 years ago poetry scriptindex

There are some things
very dear to all of us.

Living on the edge
of the sharp shores

not the first time
your pain was felt.

the last time
we spoke, there
wasn’t much to say

your downcast eyes

my trembling
heart.

yet as you returned
my lonely embrace

I was taken
by the calm
of your
sweet touch

to pass my life
in solitude.

-scriptindex, 06/03/17 mon
