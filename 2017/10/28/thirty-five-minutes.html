thirty-five minutes

Before the busy days.

Pupils dilate, morals update
Libido unstable, no tricks.

a coy yet anxious glaze
those curled, parched lips that
quiver in fright

rhythmic strokes of your
light fingertips

resist the allure
of your sweet scent

two inches away from

desperation

this creamy embrace
my treat for you.
