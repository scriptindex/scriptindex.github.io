Tea Break

08 April 2017 on poetry

Thinking about life
with a new skillset.

Lovely rooibos
gently flavoured
squirts of honey
no milk for me.

An enduring heat
constantly billowing

the shapeshifter
escapes intact.

Generous monotony
always reliable
as the hardback
remains unread.
