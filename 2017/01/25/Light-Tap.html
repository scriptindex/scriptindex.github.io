More poetic prose!

Fresh cut grass
fragrant lavender
cocoa dusted creampuffs
and chewy cupcakes.

Layers of cutlery
stacked in unison
want to break free
and cause havok.

These are a few things
you can turn into reality.

-scriptindex, 25/01/17 wed
