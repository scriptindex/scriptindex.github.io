12 February 2020 on poetry

it’s about time.

Friday 17th January 2020.

all this talk
about healing
and learning

in the new year

finally makes sense.

-

it’s been just
over two weeks

since the new year

but now,

it’s clear

this healing
and learning

I previously spoke of;

it has only
just started.

-

there are a lot
of complex things

to work through
which naturally

require a lot
of time, effort
and hard work

but first

before any
of this,

I need to get
some sleep…​

-scriptindex, 17/01/20 fri
