Bob Dylan

Saturday 9th December 2017.

Bob Dylan
We thank you

Your influence
on the medium
is quite profound

Your ways have been
eccentric, challenging
and rewarding

The stage is your haven
of musical creativity

While your life may have
been a tumultuous chain
of events that drained you
of life’s passions

We appreciate you
no matter what

Thank you
Dylan

Thank you once again.
