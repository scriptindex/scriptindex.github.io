27 Aug 2019 on poetry

promise

~Liberi Fatali.

I’ll be here…​

why?

I’ll be waiting here…​

for…​

I’ll be waiting here
for you, so…​

If you come…​

here…​

If you come here…​

then…​

You’ll find me.

why?

You will.

I promise.

-scriptindex, 27/08/19 tue
