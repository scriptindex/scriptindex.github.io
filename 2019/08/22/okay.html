22 Aug 2019 on poetry

okay

always.

we can get
in our own way

but we will try to
make it through.

people will try
to get their way

but we will surely
make it through.

life will try
its best to
test us

but we will always
make it through.

there is
no need
to worry.

everything is
going to be
okay.

-scriptindex, 22/08/19 thu
