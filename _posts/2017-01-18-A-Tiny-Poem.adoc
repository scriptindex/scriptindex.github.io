= A Tiny Poem
:hp-tags: poetry

A short bite of goodness!

*'Green Times'*

So this is one of those +
great days I've been +
hearing about.

crisp clear drops +
from the +
silent sky +

crashing down +
on faces unknown +

race inside +
to shelter +
and warmth +

clear streets +
population zero

F

This isn't a great day. +
It's just different.
