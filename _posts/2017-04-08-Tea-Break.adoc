
= Tea Break
:hp-tags: poetry

Thinking about life +
with a new skillset.

---

Lovely rooibos +
gently flavoured +
squirts of honey +
no milk for me. +

An enduring heat +
constantly billowing +

the shapeshifter +
escapes intact. +

Generous monotony +
always reliable +
as the hardback +
remains unread.
