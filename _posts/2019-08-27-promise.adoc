= promise
:hp-tags: poetry
:published-at: 2019-08-28

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nyuq2M5NjpQ[~Liberi Fatali.]

---

I'll be here... +

> why? +

I'll be waiting here... +

> for... +

I'll be waiting here +
for you, so...

If you come...

> here...

If you come here... +

> then... +

You'll find me. +

> why? +

You will. +

I promise.
