= Good Luck
:hp-tags: poetry
:published-at: 2017-12-23

Today.

---

tonight, there be +
recollection +

three six five +
pure contemplation +

as I explore these forms +
of poetical expression +

you have somewhat observed +
the result of creation +

untapped, perhaps +
a sphere in motion +

definitely rolling in +
the right direction +

tonight, there be +
celebration +

as we head towards +
our destination +

tomorrow +
is open +
to interpretation.
