= Light Tap
:hp-tags: poetry

More poetic prose!

---

Fresh cut grass +
fragrant lavender +
cocoa dusted creampuffs +
and chewy cupcakes. 

Layers of cutlery +
stacked in unison +
want to break free +
and cause havok.

These are a few things +
you can turn into reality.
